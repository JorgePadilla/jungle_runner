## Full Description

🏃 RUN THROUGH THE JUNGLE — HOW FAR CAN YOU GO?

Jungle Runner is a fast-paced endless runner where you dash through a dangerous jungle filled with obstacles, traps, and treasures.

TAP to jump over rocks, logs, and fire traps. SWIPE DOWN to slide under spinning saw blades and spike balls. Collect coins, grab power-ups, and push your high score to the limit!

🎮 FEATURES:
• Simple one-hand controls — tap to jump, swipe to slide
• 5 unique obstacles with animations — saws, fire, rock heads & more
• Beautiful parallax jungle backgrounds with 5 scrolling layers
• Unlockable characters — Ninja Frog, Virtual Guy, Mask Dude, Pink Man
• Shield & Magnet power-ups to boost your run
• Daily rewards — log in every day for bonus coins
• Missions & challenges to keep you coming back
• Progressive difficulty — the jungle gets faster the further you go

🏆 COMPETE:
• Beat your high score and push further each run
• Complete missions for coin rewards
• Unlock all characters and show off your style

🎵 AUDIO:
• Original jungle-themed soundtrack
• Satisfying sound effects for every jump, slide & coin grab

Download now and see how far you can run! 🌴
